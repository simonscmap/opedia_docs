Dataset Description¶

This dataset is a collection of Picophytoplankton abundances and biomass measurements from 75 cruises and stations around the global ocean. It contains flow cytometry derived abundance measurements for prochlorococcus, synenchoccous and picoeukaryotes as as well as total pikophytoplankton abundance and biomass. The specific cruise names used to compile the dataset are listed in Table 1. of the authors paper. Conversion factors from cell abundance to carbon biomass are listed in Table 2. (https://doi.org/10.1594/PANGAEA.777385)

Warning

20229 of the total 40946 data points were excluded from the original dataset due to invalid dates. The raw dataset can be found at https://doi.org/10.1594/PANGAEA.777385
