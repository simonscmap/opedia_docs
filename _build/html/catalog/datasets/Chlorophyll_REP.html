Dataset Description¶

The Chlorophyll_REP dataset in CMAP is a global 25km X 25km 8 day averaged product, with a temporal coverage from 1998-01-01 through 2018-06-26. This product is a satellite derived chl_a estimate for the global ocean. Data is provided by the Ocean Colour Thematic Center at the Copernicus Marine environment monitoring service (CMEMS)] The dataset is generated from ESA and NASA satellite missions, specifically from SeaWiFS, MODIS-Squa, MERIS, VIIRSN and OLCI-S3A.
